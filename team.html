Dipper Pines

A smart, curious, and adventurous 13-year-old boy. His family lives in Piedmont, California and his favorite holiday is Halloween. He is spending the summer along with his twin sister Mabel in Gravity Falls with their Great Uncle Stan.

Mabel Pines

Mabel is a 13-year-old girl spending the summer with her Great Uncle Stan in Gravity Falls, Oregon along with her brother Dipper. She takes a much less serious approach to life than her twin brother. Her first word was "unicorn".

Stanford Pines
